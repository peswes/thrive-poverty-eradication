Mission Statement

Thrive poverty eradication initiatives exists to help the deprived, excluded and vulnerable children , women widows, victims of domestic and sexual violence victims and flood disasters victims to let them have the capacity to improve their lives and the opportunity to become independent and self reliance in life

We stand by leaders who bring lasting and positive change in their communities. By partnering to promote societies who individuals and institutions participate in valuing, protecting and advancing the worth and rights of children. We partner with communities, IDP centers to help empower victims directly and indirectly with Medical supplies, shelter scholarships, school supplies and nutritional food supplies. We are determined to help victims get back up on their feet through empowerment of skills acquisition opportunities for self reliance and financial independence.

We can enrich, nurtured and support more lives through their support of our cause.
