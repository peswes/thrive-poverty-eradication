Legal Adviser: Vona Ekpebe

Vona Ekpebe is an accomplished attorney with experience representing Fortune 500 companies in complex litigation, influencer marketing, marketing, advertising, and content related matters. She has a broad background that has allowed her to evaluate and provide advice on a wide variety of legal matters, making her particularly effective as an in-house counsel. Vona has a demonstrated track record of giving solid and strategic business advice to executives and managers across the enterprise, and of developing and executing strategies that achieve business objectives and significant cost savings.

Vona has been responsible for leading influencer marketing, marketing, advertising, and content related deals, strategies, campaigns, and initiatives, as well as ensuring compliance and resolving disputes through litigation or ADR. Vona handles all facets of the litigation process, including settlement negotiation, mediation, arbitration, trial, and appellate work. Vona has also handled a wide range of contractual, transactional, and employment matters, developing a broad practice and professional background that has made her proficient in evaluating a wide variety of legal issues, and providing effective solutions to the same.

Vona has successfully advanced her clients’ interests in a wide variety of complex influencer marketing, marketing, advertising, content, and litigation matters. She has successfully drafted and negotiated key talent deals with a range of celebrities and athletes as well as major business partnerships, settled and litigated significant litigation matters including class actions, and advised on key influencer marketing, marketing, advertising campaigns and deals.

Vona earned her J.D. from UCLA School of Law (2005), where she was a Finalist in the Thurgood Marshall Mock Trial Western Regional and National Competitions. She received her B.A. with High Honors from the University of California, Santa Barbara (2002) where she studied Political Science.

She is admitted to practice in the State of California, in the United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, and in the United States District Court, Central District of California and Southern District of California.

Specialties: Influencer Marketing, Marketing, Advertising, Content, Litigation
